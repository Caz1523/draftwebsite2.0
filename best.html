1. Asante Samuel Jr.

One of my favorite players from this years draft after sitting out his last season for many he dropped down peoples boards but his talent is just too much to not translate to the NFL and to get that in the second round was a steal.

2. Christian Barmore

Defensive tackle was not a position many were looking for which is why he fell so far. Despite this for him to end up in New England with "the hoodie" just makes me think of Vince Wilfork.

3. Rashawn Slater

Mentioned before but with the priority being on the protection of Justin Herbet this offseason Slater was a dream come true.
